Guidelines

In this exercise, you'll be shown a password briefly.

Your goal is to remember it and type it correctly.

Each level gets harder by changing:

  • Length of the password
  • Types of characters used (like numbers, letters, and symbols)
  • Difficulty category, from beginner to professional
  • E: Beginner – Short passwords using only numbers
  • D: Novice – Lowercase letters only
  • C: Intermediate – Upper & lowercase letters
  • B: Advanced – Letters and numbers mixed
  • A: Professional – Letters, numbers, and symbols

Stay focused — your memory and password strength improve with every round!
